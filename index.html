S.T.E.A.M Hub for Bogoso and Beyond

Yamoransa Model Lab 8 is a dedicated center for STEM education and technology access in Bogoso, Western Region, Ghana. We are committed to bridging the digital divide and empowering individuals with the skills necessary to thrive in the modern world.
